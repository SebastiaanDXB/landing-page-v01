    HOW TO WORK THE AI

    Due to being machine-learning software, ATRICA needs good data in order to get started and thereafter consistently improve on its own data-driven intelligence to generate top-ranking keywords. To get ATRICA going, we work with clients to identify and input 250 unique Orientation Keywords (OTKs) for their website.... Think of OTKs like the foundation of your house; they’re the building blocks the AI needs to keep building higher and higher.

    Identifying and inputting the OTKs is a once-off process. After this the AI completely takes over to generate thousands of Rank-up Keywords (RuKs).

    RuKs are better, trending and more relevant keywords for your website and, as the name suggests, keep ranking the site up notch by notch.

    Conversely, ATRICA automatically discards non-performing keywords – those that are not trending or don’t get enough relevant hits – from your website.

    ATRICA does all this and more round-the-clock with zero human intervention.

    And it does it faster and better than any human could, at far less cost.
